# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the AméthMC FAQ! If you have a question about our utilities, you will likely find the answer here.

---

### General Questions

**What is AméthMC?**
AméthMC is a collection of premium, web-based Minecraft utilities created by DevFrisly. Our goal is to make setting up and optimizing your Minecraft survival experience as seamless as possible, starting with our flagship MRPACK Converter.

**Is AméthMC free to use?**
Yes! All the tools on AméthMC are 100% free. If you'd like to help keep the servers running and support development, you can [leave a tip here](https://ko-fi.com/devfrisly).

**Are you affiliated with Mojang or Modrinth?**
No. AméthMC is an independent, community-driven project. We love Modrinth and utilize their open API, but we are not officially partnered with or endorsed by them, Mojang AB, or Microsoft.

### MRPACK Converter

**Why use this converter instead of the Modrinth App?**
The Modrinth App is fantastic for playing on your own computer. However, if you are setting up a multiplayer server (like a Paper or Fabric server) or using a third-party launcher like MultiMC, you often need the actual `.jar` files. Our converter extracts the `.mrpack` file and downloads all the `.jar` files for you instantly in your browser.

**Do you store my `.mrpack` files or the downloaded mods?**
No. The entire conversion process happens locally in your browser and temporarily in memory. We do not save your modpacks, track which mods you download, or store any of the generated `.zip` files on our servers. Your privacy is 100% respected.

**Can I convert CurseForge `.zip` modpacks here?**
Currently, the converter only supports Modrinth `.mrpack` files.
